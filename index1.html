About Us


Hiree.io is a tech based integrated platform for Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Cost Accountants, Lawyers, Certified Financial Accountants, Valuation experts and other Financial Experts to showcase their expertise, increase their network and get multiple clients. Hiree.io is a very easy to use service platform where small and medium sized businesses, startups, entrepreneurs can start and manage their business and finances at very affordable cost and have multiple finance experts to choose from anywhere around the world. We provide hi-tech platform which helps in quick and suitable matches, quick introduction of service providers, easy appointment bookings and online task tracking through our fully automated software. Our platform provides verified leads and assignments to the experts and dedicated support system for both the clients and the experts.
